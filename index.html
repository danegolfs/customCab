About Custom Cabinetry Solutions

Custom Cabinetry Solutions provides beauty and strength in the finest made cabinets at very attractive prices.

Our cabinets are crafted by an Amish factory in rural Indiana, and they are delivered directly to your job site. The woods are top quality and the finishes are both stunning and durable. Whether a small room remodel or an entire house of new construction, we professionally design, plan and install cabinets that provide eye-catching good looks and proven practicality.

Soft close actuation, smooth ball bearing movement with full extension under-mount slides result in comfortable use and quiet operation. Exactly the features you should expect in a high-end product.

Our cabinets are constructed with 3/4 inch sturdy plywood boxes and a wide variety of beautiful hardwood exteriors from rustic knotty hickory to sleek, painted slab styles for a more modern touch. Attractive moldings and accents can be selected in a variety of finishes, and we can expertly match any desired stain hue or color. Our woods are expertly crafted and meticulously finished for a lifetime of rugged use.

With almost limitless options for door panels and drawer faceplate designs, you will be able to pick exactly the look you want knowing the quality and construction will be of the highest caliber. No matter what your taste, style and flair, we will help your dream project become a happy reality, one you are anxious to show off to your family and friends for years to come.
